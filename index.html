We Are
Piotr and Ash International Private Ltd

Piotr and Ash is an endeavour to promote high technology and latest equipment in the Indian sub-continent with a strong focus on dairy and food industry.

Co-founded by two ex-Managing Directors of the GEA Group, one based in India and the other in Europe, Piotr And Ash has the advantage of a great network within the Indian dairy and food market, together with the desired know-how and support of European and American technology.
